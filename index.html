Since its inception in 2000, the ISMIR community mailing list has grown to over 1,800 members from across the world, and routinely receives announcements about conferences, career opportunities, concerts, and wide variety of other issues relevant to music information retrieval:

  • Community Announcements: To become a member, proceed to the group and select "Apply to join group", or send an email to "community+subscribe@ismir.net". Viewing or posting topics is open to the public; note, however, that non-member messages are screened.
